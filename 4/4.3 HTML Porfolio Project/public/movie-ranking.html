The Best Movies According to Geoff

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Big Lebowski

A great movie about a normal guy that is mistakenly targeted due to having the same name as a very rich man.

Oh Brother! Where Art Thou

A creative retelling of the Odyssey by Homer that is set in the depression era South.

The Matrix

A Science Fiction movie about waking up from a similated world.
